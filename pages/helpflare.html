Help Flare

Node.js | MongoDB | AWS

Overview

During the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic lockdown in India, good Samaritans and NGOs stepped up to help under-privileged localities with basic needs like food, water, sanitation, etc.

To help their efforts, I along with my classmates created an app that used Geo-fencing technology to highlight communities that were in need.

The motive of this project was to contribute to the society and to the man-kind using the most available luxury i.e technology. The issues faced by the people were lack of daily necessities. To solve it, we came up with an approach which gives a broad field of view to the social groups/clubs or NGOs and would make their help deliveries faster to more people in the coverage.

Approach
  • - Any citizen of the locality, reports an area where help is needed, to the application with data such as number of families, resources needed and details of the reporter.
  • - This area is marked on a Google Map to which any group registered, can deliver the help.
  • - These groups then contact the reporter of that area for more clear details and distribute the essentials.
Role

My responsibilities in the project were to build the APIs and model the data which was to be stored in MongoDB. Authentication, Report creation, Geo-fencing & Confirmation of help delivered, were the core features of the application.

To cater to a larger reach, Help Flare got featured into newspapers like Times of India, Hindustan Times and an educational digest known as Higher Educational Digest. Although, we could only help a few people around us, the purpose was served i.e to help the needy, in a crisis which we all went through.
